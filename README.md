The bar chart displays the average years of experience required by job level, revealing a clear progression: Executive roles require the most experience (around 14â€“15 years), followed by Director, Mid-Senior, Associate, Entry Level, and Internship positions.

Overall, the report shows that the job market is strongly oriented toward experienced and senior-level professionals, with fewer opportunities at entry-level, and that experience requirements increase consistently with job seniority.
